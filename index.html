About us

Your neighbourhood
store for everything.

2 Fantastic Kaiapoi is a locally-owned variety and gift store on Hilton Street, right next to CBK, McDonald's and Woolworths. We stock a huge range of affordable gifts, toys, stationery, cosmetics, party supplies, and kitchen essentials — all in one place. Whether you need a last-minute birthday gift or a full party set-up, we've got you covered.

Always something new in store Our range is always evolving — come in regularly and you'll always find something fresh.
